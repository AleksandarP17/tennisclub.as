About Us 🎾

Welcome to Tennis Club AS, the home of passionate tennis players and future champions! Founded in 2025, our club is dedicated to providing top-quality training, exciting tournaments, and premium court rentals for players of all levels.

At Tennis Club AS, we offer:

  • Professional Training – Whether you're a beginner or an advanced player, our experienced coaches will help you improve your game.
  • Competitive Tournaments – We organize regular tournaments for all age groups, creating a fun and challenging environment.
  • Court Rentals – Need a place to play? Our high-quality courts are available for rent, ensuring the best playing experience.
